The King James Version is based on documents available to the translators in 1611. They researched several sources to create a good almost word-for-word translation.. . The Living Bible was done in 1971 by Keneth Taylor. He, almost singlehandedly, created a version of the Bible for children, etc. created by paraphrasing the King James bit by bit.. . The Living Bible has recieved much criticism for being a paraphrase and not adhering well to the original texts in many places, however it was very popular among youth groups in the late 70's.. . It was greatly overhauled in 1996 as the New Living Bible- considered better.
