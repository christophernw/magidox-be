That could happens in some fans because they come with a special  switch to  change the rotation of the blades. The  warm air always are near the ceiling where the fans is and the cold air is at the floor level.During the winter what the fan do is  generate  a circulation path from  top to bottom like a clockwise movement from the floor to the roof bringing  the heat to the floor; on the  summer, when you switch the rotation,  you invert the process. When you go to the store you can see a diagram on the side of the box that illustrate this...
