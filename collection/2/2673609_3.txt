This is my Italian mother's recipe for meatballs. Everyone always loves them and you can adjust the seasonings to taste.. . INGREDIENTS:. .     * 1 lb lean ground beef.     * 1/2 cup Italian bread crumbs.     * 1/3 cup water (more or less).     * 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese.     * 1 medium onion.     * garlic.     * salt.     * pepper . . PREPARATION:. Mix all ingredients together. Don't add all of the water at one time. Mixture should be moist but not so that the meatballs fall apart.. . Shape meatballs to desired size and place on a broiler pan (I wet my hands before shaping each meatball and it helps make a nice meatball.) Broil until outside is slightly brown on one side and then turn and broil the other side.. When finished, add to spaghetti sauce and simmer at least 20 minutes. ---------------------. Easy Italian meatballs recipe. This meatball recipe can be used in spaghetti, in a meatball sandwich, or as an appetizer.. . 1 pound ground beef. . 1/2 pound ground turkey. . salt and pepper to taste. . 1 tablespoon parsley. . 1 tablespoon nutmeg. . 1 tablespoon thyme. . 2 eggs. . Mix all ingredients except eggs. Add eggs and mix until thoroughly combined. Roll in flour to form 1 inch balls. In a large skillet, heat shortening then add meatballs, and cook until browned, about 5 minutes over medium heat. Lower heat and cook an additional 25 minutes, turning occasionally.
