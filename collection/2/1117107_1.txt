The owner's manual will not tell you this.  If you are at all mechanically inclined, have (or can borrow) a basic set of metric ratchets, and/or are not afraid to get your hands dirty (and possibly get a little frustrated) get a Chilton (best) or Haynes manual for the make and year of the car (from your library if it's a good one, or an auto parts store (you can also get the pump and gaskets while you are there!)) and dig in.
