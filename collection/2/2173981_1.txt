Vitamin B12 injections are indicated for certain anemias, gastrointestinal disorders, and neuropathies.. . Vitamin B12 is extracted from the liver and is essential to red blood cell production. With inadequate absorption, a deficiency results in pernicious or other megaloblastic anemias and severe neurologic symptoms secondary to peripheral nerve and spinal cord damage (combined system disease) may result.. . As far as I have read, the only side effect I could find was a rare allergic reaction in very few patients.
