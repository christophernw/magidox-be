RuneScape is an MMORPG (massive multiplayer online role-playing computer game) implemented in Java, with over 2.8 million registered players. RuneScape was launched by Jagex Ltd. on January 4, 2001 and offers both pay to play ("P2P") and free to play ("F2P") membership options to players..  . The Runescape website's homepageRunescape allows players to develop many different non-combat skills ranging from runecrafting and mining to cooking and crafting. The large range of non-combat skills means that Runescape has an expansive, dynamic player economy.
