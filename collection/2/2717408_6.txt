First, find out the root cause for their desire to leave. Ask them. Even if they aren't talking about leaving, a good manager finds out what his GOOD employees need in order to stay with the company, and then he gives that to those employees.. . Do some research in your area regarding salary and benefits. Make sure you are at LEAST running average. I suggest you run higher than average.. . If your salary and benefits are above average and your employees are working in a comfortable, enjoyable work environment, it is highly unlikely they'll even take a visit to monster.com. Spend some time finding out what they need from you. As a manager, this is your job. If you have a Human Resources department, work closely with them to solve this issue.
