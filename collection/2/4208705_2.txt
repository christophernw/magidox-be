Important Tips for Writing History Papers . 1. Write in the simple past tense.. 2. Avoid excessive use of the passive voice.. 3. Avoid the use of the pronoun “I.” . 4. Avoid the use of qualifying terms.. 5. Vary sentence structure.. 6. Be sure pronouns agree with their antecedent.. 7. Avoid slang. . 8. Omit needless words.. 9. Know your audience.. 10. Read your draft aloud.. 11. Cite sources properly.
