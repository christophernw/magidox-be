HARD-BOILED EGGS. . 1.  Cold water at least 1 inch about egg(s). . 2.  Heat to boiling in saucepan; remove from heat.  Cover and let stand 18 minutes.  Immediately cool briefly in cold water to prevent further cooking.  Tap egg to crack shell; roll egg between hands to loosen shell, then peel.. . 3.  If shell is hard to peel, hold egg in cold water while peeling.
