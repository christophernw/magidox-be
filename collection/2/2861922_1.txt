White phosphorus is one of the 3 allotropic forms of phosphorus. Red, White and Black. . . White phosphorus is extracted from types of phosphate rock by heating in a really, really hot oven to give phosphorus pentoxide then reducing to give white phosphorus. . . White phosphorus can then be heated again or put underwater and left out in the sun to give Red Phosphorus.. . White phosphorus can also be heated with a Mercury catalyst to give Black Phosphorus.. . See the wiki for info and uses of each type.. http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phosphorus#Forms
