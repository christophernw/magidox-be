Like the mechanic previously said, it is very uncommon for this type of work to be needed. However, if you are sure it is necessary, the procedure would be as follows.. 1) Remove upper intake manifold (drain coolant first). 2) Remove valve covers. 3) Remove fuel injectors & all related electronics from lower intake manifold. 4) Remove lower intake manifold. 5) Loosen rocker arm nuts enough to allow pushrods to come out (so you can move rocker arms aside) and then remove pushrods. 6) Reverse the process to reassemble but make sure you torque everything to spec
