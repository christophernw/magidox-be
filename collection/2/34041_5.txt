Common symptoms of strep throat in children and adults include:. .     * Severe and sudden sore throat without coughing, sneezing, or other coldlike symptoms..     * Pain or difficulty with swallowing..     * Fever over 101 °F(38.3 °C); lower fevers may indicate a viral infection and not strep..     * Swollen lymph nodes in the neck..     * White or yellow spots or coating on the throat and tonsils..     * Bright red throat or dark red spots on the roof of the mouth at the back near the throat..     * Swollen tonsils, although this symptom may also be caused by a viral infection.
