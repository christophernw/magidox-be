Microclimate Technologies International provides solutions for precise control of humidity, temperature, and pollution in enclosed volumes. Their active microclimate control devices are trusted by museums, archives, and laboratories around the world. Microclimates are used for exhibition cases, and archive and artefact storage by the world's major museums.. . What is a microclimate? . A microclimate is the environment immediately surrounding, and interacting with, an artefact. . . Why create a microclimate? . Modification of a microclimate is usually easier, safer, more accurate, and more cost-effective than solutions that involve modifying the environment in an entire museum, gallery, or room. . .
