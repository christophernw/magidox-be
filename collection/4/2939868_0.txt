Marketing mix refers to utilizing many marketing outlets, such as tradeshows, advertising, brochures, etc. PR is technically not in a marketing mix, but it is similar in its promotional nature, smaller companies will often rely on marketing departments to generate PR since they are the creative arm of a company.. . PR is like free advertising, it only costs you labor to write and distribute press releases, if the release gets run in the media it is does not cost any thing.. . Nothing in the marketing mix is free.
