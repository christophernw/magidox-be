Optometry, like the name suggests deals with the fine nuances of measuring, analysing the pictorial interpretation of the human eye and then converting it into mathematical interpretation ,meaning how much power lens the patient needs ,short sightedness etc. You work symbiotically with opthalmalogists,somewhat like a support..    Optometry is a rage in North America and has good prospects in eye clinics, eye centres etc, also lens manufacturing firms ..     You should have a strong background in mathematics, optical geometry to gain admission into this course, insight into biology is also an extra added arsenal to your qualification.
