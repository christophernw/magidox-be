A spear is a pole with a sharp head, usually used for stabbing and thrusting.  A javelin is a light pole with a sharp end for throwing at a target, now largely used in sport.  Spear might be though of as a general category of weapon under which specific weapons of size and use, (sarissa, habelards, pilums, pikes) all under this heading - some handled (pikes,) some thrown (pilum) For a full listing of spear types try here:. http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spear
