Curse words exist only in context. The words we know in English as curse words didn't start out that way. These words have just gathered increasingly negative meaning over time. English is a fluid language, and thus, its it more open to new words and changes in meaning of words. Other languages probably have words that carry strong negative meaning, but U.S. English differs in how it changes rapidly, stresses individuality, rarely pays attention to grammar and assimilates the words of other languages. . . Yes, most languages do have words that carry the same negative meaning as english curse words, it just depends on context and the culture of the particular language
