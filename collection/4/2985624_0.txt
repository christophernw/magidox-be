Darkness (pinkness, not any other color) around a cut is normal.  You can tell if it is infected by the following signs:. .     * Increased pain, swelling, redness, or warmth around the affected area..     * Red streaks extending from lymph nodes in the affected area toward the body..     * Drainage of pus from the area..     * Swollen lymph nodes in the neck, armpit, or groin..     * Fever.
