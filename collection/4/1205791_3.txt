The Queen sends messages only for very special occassions. Examples of this would be for Diamond (sixtieth), sixty-fifth and Platinum (seventieth) wedding anniversaries and then every year thereafter. Her Majesty does not send a message for Silver (twenty-fifth), Ruby (fortieth) or Golden (fiftieth) Wedding anniversaries.. . She also sends them for special birthdays: one hundredth and one hundred and fifth birthdays and then every year thereafter.. .
