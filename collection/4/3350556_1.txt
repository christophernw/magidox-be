fast runners aren't the same as distance runners. So don't just do LSD (long, slow distances) and think that will make you faster. To get faster:. . run sprints. . do intervals - basically run hard for 30 seconds, then jog for a minute, run hard for 30 secs, jog for a minute.. repeat this 8-12 times in a workout. . learn how to lift weights and learn how to do explosive (olympic style) weightlifting safely. Ever notice how GREAT sprinters look? Make or female they have great, cut bodies. Learn how to squat, snatch, deadlift, and overhead press. It'll make you faster.
