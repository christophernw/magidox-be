First, know the audience.. Second, know the subject.. Third, know how to approach the audience with what you know on the subject.. Fourth, decide what you want the audience to do with what you tell them of what you know on the subject.. Fifth, eliminate the distracting and inconsequential from what you would or could say, and rephrase or rearrange what is most important of what you will say.. Finally, deliver it.  Deliver it from your soul, from your heart, from your passion, from your purpose.. . Whether others could do better, when you make it the best you can honestly deliver, it will be enough.
