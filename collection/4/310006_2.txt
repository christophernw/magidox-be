She should have been on a premium adult food prior to pregnancy and for the first few weeks of pregnancy. Starting the fourth week of pregnancy, begin adding a premium kitten food to her diet. Each week increase the amount of the kitten food, so when she is in her final week of pregnancy, she is on all kitten food. Increase the frequency of the daily meals to three by mid-pregnancy or free feed her. She may need to eat small meals every 3-4 hours during the last week of the pregnancy as the kittens continue to take up more room.
