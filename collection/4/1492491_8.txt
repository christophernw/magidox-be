Tea, INDIAN STYLE. . Ingreidients:-. . Water half cup. Milk half cup. Sugar 1 tsp.. Tea 1 small tsp.. Ginger about an inch piece finely chopped.. . Pour water in to pan and turn on the heat, and tea first and then sugar, then add ginger, wait until it boils and then slow the heat add milk, again turn heat to full and wait until the tea boils. Strain and serve
