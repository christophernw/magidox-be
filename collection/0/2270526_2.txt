Marketing is the activities of a company associated with buying and selling a product or service. It includes advertising, selling and delivering products to people. People who work in marketing departments of companies try to get the attention of target audiences by using slogans, packaging design, celebrity endorsements and general media exposure.. . Advertising, on the other hand, is the activity of attracting public attention to a product or business, as by paid announcements in the print, broadcast, or electronic media.
