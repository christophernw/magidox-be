Kentucky Fried Chicken changed to KFC in 1991 to avoid the unhealthy connotations of fried food. It also wanted to widen its range outside chickens and because there was a trend towards shorter names. In 2004, it further tried to rebrand itself as "Kitchen Fresh Chicken although this claim did not apply to chicken wings. . . There are claims on the Internet that the name was changed because they use genetically engineered animals that can no longer be called chickens. This claim is false.
