The Nervous System has FOUR FUNCTIONS that enable the body to respond quickly.  The Nervous System: . .     A. Gathers information both from the outside world and from inside the body. SENSORY FUNCTION . .     B. Transmits the information to the processing area of the brain and spinal cord. . .     C.  Processes the information to determine the best response.  INTEGRATIVE FUNCTION . .     D.  Sends information to muscles, glands, and organs (effectors) so they can respond correctly.  Muscular contraction or glandular secretions.  MOTOR FUNCTION . .
