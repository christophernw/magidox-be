cajun is aterm used for an ethnic group that is comprised of mixed races of spanish, german,french,native indian populations. this group is found in the southern part of state of louisiana,usa. this particular group's ancestors came from quebec, canada ; and from france. they were forced to leave their homeland in 1755 in what is now nova scotia, canada area and move to the southern part of united states. they speak a language that is a dialect of french language.the cajun food is  seasoned, sometimes spicy and hearty. their religion is primarily roman catholic.. regards. bhalinder
