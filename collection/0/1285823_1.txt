The literal translation of "Je ne sais quoi" to English is indeed "I don't know what"! But it is normally used to point out something which is usually undefinable: For instance, if you say someone has a certain "je ne sais quoi", then you find that person has a very particular characteristic, which you like (or not!) but that cannot define with other words. The expression is also used by "gourmets" referring to exquisite food...
