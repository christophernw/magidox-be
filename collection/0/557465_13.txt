Cake Mix Buttermilk Chocolate Cake. . Duncan Hines or plain store brand chocolate cake mix (NOT with pudding). 1 tablespoon cocoa (NOT hot chocolate mix). 1 1/3 cups buttermilk. 1/2 cup oil. 1 teaspoon vanilla or almond extract. 3 eggs. Beat together per pkg directions.  Pour into greased 9 x 13 cake pan.  Bake at 350 for 40 minutes until cake tests done.. Frost with your favorite home made frosting - there is no substitute for a good home made frosting.. (you can use Saco brand dehydrated buttermilk in this recipe)
