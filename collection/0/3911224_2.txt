After the Kuwait Invasion, Iraq had to demilitarize.. ---Iraq’s disarmament could not be verified, and Iraq did not cooperate with weapons inspectors. The economic connection between the US and Iraq was oil, until the 2003 Invasion of Iraq. A report by Swedish diplomat Hans Blix “stated that Iraqis capability in producing weapons of mass destruction was not significantly different from 1992 when the country dismantled the bulk of their remaining arsenals under terms of the cease-fire agreement with U.N. forces, but did not completely rule out the possibility that Saddam still had such weapons. The United States and the United Kingdom charged that Iraq was hiding weapons,”. After these allegations, the Invasion of Iraq happened, and the insurgency began.---
