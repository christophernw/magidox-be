ros·trum (rŏs'trəm, rô'strəm) . n., pl. ros·trums or ros·tra (rŏs'trə, rô'strə).. A dais, pulpit, or other elevated platform for public speaking.. . The curved, beaklike prow of an ancient Roman ship, especially a war galley.. The speaker's platform in an ancient Roman forum, which was decorated with the prows of captured enemy ships.. Biology. A beaklike or snoutlike projection.
