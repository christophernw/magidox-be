Actually, the Battle of Gettysburg was a decisive Union victory. Many historians call the Battle of Gettysburg the turning point in the Civil War.  It was one of the bloodiest battles of the War, Union General George Meade defeated the Confederate General Lee's army.  Among famous failed attacks on the Confederate side was Pickett's Charge in which Lee commanded General George Pickett to try and take the Union's position on Cemetery Ridge, but resulted in a disastrous defeat.
