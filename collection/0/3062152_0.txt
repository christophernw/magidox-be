CFNM, or Clothed Female(s), Naked (or Nude) Male(s) is a term for a paraphilia involving one or more nude men and one or more clothed women. It is generally used as a label for pictorial erotica or pornography, or a sexual fantasy depicting such a scenario.. . The general category of "CFNM" is fairly broad, encompassing several different types of scenario.
