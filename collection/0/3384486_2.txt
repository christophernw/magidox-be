Agricultural lands are located very near the rivers and  lakes in view of the necessity for  irrigation, labour and the markets for agricultural commodities  are also located near the centres. Since transport of the bulk of the produce.is difficult the agro based industries are also located near the agri-markets or near the transport centres..              In case of the minerals the raw material, the ore, coal, mines, electricity ,  water supply are very important for the industries  and they are located near such  centres.
