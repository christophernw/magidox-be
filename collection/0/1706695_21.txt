Some people don't trust debit cards.  Some people like the fact that the returned check (or bank scan of a check) is also a receipt!  Many older folks just grew up using checks and are totally comfortable with them.  . . Besides, if an error is made on the debit card transaction and you are charged too much, or twice, it's a battle between you and the bank.  With a check, it's easy.  Break out your copy and there is your proof.
