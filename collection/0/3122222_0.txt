Peripheral neuropathy is a term that decribes a disorder of, or damage to, the peripheral nervous system. The peripheral nervous system consists of all the motor and sensory nerves that connect the brain and spinal cord to the rest of the body (i.e., the nerves outside the central nervous system).. . Most often, symptoms such as pain, tingling, and/or muscle weakness start in both of the feet, and progress up the legs. This is usually followed by symptoms in the hands that progress up the arms.
