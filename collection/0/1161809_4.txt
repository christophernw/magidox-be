You're spoiling her by letting her control the situation. Don't allow her water from anywhere but her bowl. Make sure the bowl is clean, and put nice cold water in it. Eventually, she's going to cave and start drinking it. Chihuahuas are manipulative. They dominate you by getting you to do what they want. Like pick them up and carry them around, and baby them. So once you take control and make yourself the boss, the dog will start acting like a dog is supposed to.
