Don't focus on the weight gain.... Muscle weighs more than fat. If you are toned and healthy is what counts. As you work out more, the muscle with replace the fat, your weight may not change or even go up. The type of work outs you do also affect the gains. Heavy weights with low reps will build mass and make you bigger. Low weights High reps give you definition.  If you are trying to stay the same size or get smaller yet more defined, do lower weight Higher rep training.
