If you mean" Prego", in general not only does it mean  "please" when you are asking something, it also means "my pleasure" or" It is a pleasure" after having been thanked.. The expresion is also used when they answer a phone call where we would say "Hello!" and it is the first person (present  tense) of the verb to pray as in "prego a chiesa" which means "I pray in church" . The Prado is the famous Spanish art gallery in Madrid.
