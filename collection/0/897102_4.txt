While LEXAPRO is well tolerated by most people, it is important to keep in mind that medicines can affect each person differently. Some may experience no or very minor side effects, while others may find the drug more difficult to tolerate. Most of the side effects experienced by patients taking LEXAPRO are mild to moderate and go away with continued treatment, and usually do not cause patients to stop taking LEXAPRO.. . The most common adverse events reported with LEXAPRO vs placebo (approximately 5% or greater and approximately 2X placebo) were nausea, insomnia, ejaculation disorder, somnolence, increased sweating, fatigue, decreased libido, and anorgasmia.. . Less common side effects have also been reported and may include a variety of very rare side effects.  Check with your doctor if you develop any new or unusual symptoms.. . If you experience any of the following side effects, continue taking LEXAPRO and talk to your doctor: . . headache, nervousness, or anxiety . nausea, diarrhea, dry mouth, or changes in appetite or weight . sleepiness or insomnia . decreased sex drive, impotence, or difficulty having an orgasm . Side effects other than those listed here may also occur. For a list of side effects, see the LEXAPRO package insert.
