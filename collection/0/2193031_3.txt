The DVD has wide screen, pan scan, and little box. It's Digital Audio Outputs, the optical and the RCA. DVD has different formats of audio, like Dolby Digital, THX, DolbyProLogic. The VCR has two formats of video, full screen and Wide screen. VCR has Analog Outputs, coaxial and RCA. To connect a VCR you need a coaxial cable, from VCR to T.V. or a pair of RCA. For the DVD you need analog RCA, Digital S-Video or HDTV (High Definition Television).
