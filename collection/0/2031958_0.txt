The etf PXE tracks companys that research alternative fuels. . "The investment seeks investments results that correspond generally to the price and yield (before fees and expenses) of an equity index called the Dynamic Energy Exploration & Production Intellidex. The fund will normally invest at least 80% of its total assets in common stocks of energy and exploration companies, and at least 90% of its total assets in common stocks that comprise the Energy Exploration & Production Intellidex. The index is comprised of stocks of 30 U.S. companies involved in the exploration and production of natural resources used to produce energy."
