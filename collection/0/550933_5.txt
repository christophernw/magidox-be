Body of the letter:. First Paragraph - State the outstanding qualities of the school and the scholarship program. Mention also where you have learned of the scholarship program.. . Second Paragraph - State your desire to apply for scholarship. Mention why you deserve the scholarship and what you can offer the school as a scholar and after you graduate.. . Third Paragraph - Say thank you for the time spent in reading your letter. Provide your contact number in case the school needs to contact you.. .
