You need to have car insurance. Basic house insurance also in case you're broken into or a pipe bursts and floods the place, ruining it. . . Life insurance is imperative if you have children. You need to think about what would happen to them if you died.  If you don't have kids then there's no point. . . Also if you have mortgage, loans etc, could your family pay them off if you died?. . These are things you need to think about.  If you live on your own with no dependants then there's not as much risk.
