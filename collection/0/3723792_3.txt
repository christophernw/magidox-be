PayPal is a company owned by eBay which claims to be FDIC insured to hold funds like a bank but is not one. They are a Payment service that serves as a mediator between buyers and sellers. . . PayPal withdraws money from your checking account, debit or credit card upon your request with an intent to pay someone and hold  the funds in their own account until the seller claims it and they transfer the funds to their own bank accounts or leave it on their PayPal accounts.. . PayPal charges a nominal fee to the sellers for this service. The company has been sue many times over the years for keeping funds and freezing up accounts from sellers unable them to claim their money.
