I haven't tried this on a skunked pet, but it gets pet stains and odors out of carpets beautifully and it's supposed to work for skunk odors as well.  . . Mix one quart of hydrogen peroxide with 1/4 cup of baking soda and a teaspoon of liquid soap.  Pour the mixture over the cat in the bathtub, work it into the fur.  Then rinse and wash with a regular pet shampoo.. . A tip for bathing.  Sometimes a bath mat with open spaces or something similar that the cat can dig it's claws into to give it purchase makes them less nervous and easier to deal with in the tub.
