Because they are portions of portions.  Think about what is being "said" by this:  .5 x .25.  In english, it is saying one-half of a quarter, or a quarter of a half. The magnitude of the resultant portion will always be less than the original portions' magnitudes, because it has to be, because you are dividing (Z x .5 = Z/2).. . In the case of .5 x .25, division would be (1/2)/4.  It is simply further reduction of the original magnitude.
