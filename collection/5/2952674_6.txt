'ho' is a mispronunciation of 'whore', shortening the word. This may be due to ebonics, or to laziness, or to pride (regarding precision of diction as unmany or undesirable or to be mocked.). . Some words change their meaning as people attach different meanings to them over time. It is a matter of usage and associations.. .
