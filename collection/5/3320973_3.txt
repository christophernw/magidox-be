The mouthparts of arachnids are clawlike, while those of insects are jawlike. Also, insects have antennae but arachnids do not. The most obvious difference between the two is that arachnids have eight legs and insects have six.. All insects have three body parts: head, abdomen, and throax.  Insects are further divide up based on certain criteria, type of mouth parts, number of wings, type of wings, etc.
