blood·y  . adj. blood·i·er, blood·i·est . -Stained with blood. . -Of, characteristic of, or containing blood. . -Accompanied by or giving rise to bloodshed: a bloody fight. . -Bloodthirsty. . -Suggesting the color of blood; blood-red. . Chiefly British Slang. Used as an intensive: “Everyone wants to have a convict in his bloody family tree” (Robert Hughes). . www.dictionary.com. . Bloody as a slang word is awfully English.
