People respond to sugar differently.. . In some people, sugar triggers mood swings.. Energy levels may fluctuate, initially bringing on a rush of energy (shown as hyperactivity in children), followed by a "crash" into lethargy and depression.. . Others don't get the rush; they just feel sluggish and sleepy after consuming sugar.. . Some people don't notice any physical or mental effects at all.. . Sugar is also bad for your teeth, may aggravate asthma and may raise triglyceride levels. High-sugar diets may drive obesity and high blood pressure and increase risks of developing type II diabetes.
