Start out by educating yourself, there are many ways to make money on the stock market and the best thing to do in the beginning is read.  Later start by putting together a ficticious portfolio.  That is, say your intention is to invest $10,000 dollars some time in the future.  then pretend to buy some stocks or funds and follow their performance.  Factor in the cost of trading in evaluating your performance and then see whether you out performed the major indices.  Then you have some idea if your choices were good.  Look into other things like options trading and also day trading where the rules are different
