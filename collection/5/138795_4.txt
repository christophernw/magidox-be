Most doctors say around 11-14.  It also depends on the parents, if they feel their children are ready for the responsibility of contact lenses, then maybe they are.  Your eye doctor will choose the best contact lens for your eye in relation to shape and size, as well how healthy the lens is for your eye.. . Side note- you should never sleep in contact lenses, even if the FDA has approved them for it.  You more than double your chances of an eye infection, or worse, a corneal ulcer, which can cause permanent vision loss.
