An alcoholic is a person that let's his,(or her), drinking interfere with the normal daily life. For example- you drink instead of going to work-you drink despite protests from others who see your drinking as a problem-you drink to get drunk and not to be social-you drink into blackouts and don't remember what you did the night before-you drink to stop pain,or anxiety,or feelings of loneliness,or to feel braver. There are many things to point to you being an alcoholic.
