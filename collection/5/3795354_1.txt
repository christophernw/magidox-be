A cupcake (or fairy cake) is a small cake designed to serve one person, usually made in a small paper cup container. As with larger cakes, frosting and decorations are usually applied.. . The muffin may have started out as a form of small cake, or possibly an adaptation of cornbread. Early versions of these muffins tend to be less sweet and much less varied in ingredients than their contemporary forms.. . So I guess a cupcake is a sweet dessert item and the muffin is more of a breakfast food or side dish.
