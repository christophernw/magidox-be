What the human eye perceives as a colored point actually consists of three tiny spots, of red, green and blue color respectively, each having a different intensity; the combination of these RGB colored spots define the color and the brightness of the image's point.. If you'll look with a magnifier at any CRT, you'll see all over the RGB spots which, from distance, appear as colored points of the image. . If you want a more detailed answer, please let me know..
