Coloring eggs is a Pagan tradition performed during Ostara. It is one step of many in consecrating eggs in the honor of the Mother Goddess' maiden form that brings the spring. Ostara is traditionally celebrated on the Spring Equinox, which either falls on or near March 21. Christians stole this Pagan tradition, along with others, to build the foundations of their celebration of Easter.. .
