The journey of the hero Odysseus from the battle of Troy back to his house when the battle ends (this is where the story starts).  He displeases the god Poseiden by killing the cyclops (which is posieden's favorite son) so Poseiden won't let Odysseus get home.  He finally does after lots of years and he comes home to find suitors trying to claim his wife Penelope.  With the help of his son and his maid, he kills all the suitors and they live happily ever after.
