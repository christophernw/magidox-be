"Cold coffee mixed with milk, half-and-half, and chocolate liqueur. Serve over ice." . Original recipe yield: 8 servings.. Prep Time:10 Minutes. Cook Time:20 . Minutes Ready In:4 Hours 30 . MinutesServings:8 (change) . . INGREDIENTS:. 1 1/2 quarts brewed coffee, room temperature . 1 cup milk . 1 cup half-and-half cream . 1/3 cup white sugar . 1 teaspoon vanilla extract . 2 tablespoons creme de cacao . . DIRECTIONS:. In a pitcher, combine cooled coffee, milk and half-and-half. Stir in sugar, vanilla and creme de cacao. Chill in refrigerator until ready to serve.
