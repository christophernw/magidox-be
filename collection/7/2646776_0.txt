Piperidine is the common name for an organic compound with the molecular formula C5H11N that is a cyclic amine with a six-membered ring containing five carbon atoms and one nitrogen atom.  Each of the carbon atoms is also connected to two hydrogens and the nitrogen is connected to one hydrogen.. Bromobenzene is an organic compound with the molecular formula C6H5Br.  It is a benzene ring (six carbons in a ring, each with one hydrogen) in which one of the hydrogen atoms has been replaced by a bromine atom.
