A lemon and a small rod of zinc will work. If you know the Ph of the lemon, and the surface area of the rod that is in contact with the lemon you'll have enough information for your equation.. . Stick the rod into the lemon, use an alligator clip on the rod, and stick a wire into the lemon about 1/2 inch away from the rod. Attach another alligator clip to the wire. Get a voltage meter, and hook it up to the wire and rod.
