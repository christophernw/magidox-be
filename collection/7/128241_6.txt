Rewards.  Jesus used both.  He warned of hell.  A lot.  But it was in the context of a "reward tactic."  That is, He was saying, "you could have and be so much more than this.  I don't want you to miss out."  A large part of the punishment was, actually, missing out on rewards.  Jesus spoke of rewards more than any other man in the Bible.  I think He understood human nature.  We were meant to be inspired to be great.  It's false humility to say, "Oh, no, I don't need rewards.  God alone is enough for me...". .
