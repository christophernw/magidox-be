Allergies - a reaction of a sensitive person to food, pets or pollen. Food allergens are egg, chicken, chocolates sea foods. It can present as itchiness on your skin and can be mild or severe, acute or chronic. It can also present as asthma or diarrhea (milk intolerance). . Infections- cause by bacteria, viruses or fungi. Microorganisms entered our body and our response will be fever. We can have some abnormal discharge like productive cough or colds. Sometimes this will be in the form of pus or abscess. Usually the color is yellowish to greenish. There is also some degree of pain depending on the location (cellulitis, urine infection). . . Hope this helps.
