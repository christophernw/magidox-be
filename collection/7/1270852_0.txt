"Rooster" is a popular single by the grunge group Alice in Chains. The single came out in 1993 and is featured on their second official full-length album Dirt. The song was written for guitarist Jerry Cantrell's father, who served in the Vietnam War.. . The lyrics of this song features two points of view in regards to the war - one from the American soldiers' point of view, and the other from the point of view of the Vietnamese.
