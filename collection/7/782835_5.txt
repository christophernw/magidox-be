You have to have real, personal meaning for the things you are trying to learn.. . Think about the things you do remember... good times and bad... there are strong emotions attached to them.. . If you are trying to learn Spanish, you are trying to learn a new way to communicate. So, learn the Spanish vocabulary that is about the things you already are interested in. Find Spanish-language chat sites that are about your interests.. . For your test tomorrow, stop worrying about memorizing a list of words or grammar. Instead, pretend you are actually talking with someone about these things. Make up sentences using your vocabulary that you need to learn, as part of your pretend conversation.
