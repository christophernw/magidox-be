Diabetes has two primary causes involved:. . 1) Genetics. . If you have a family history of diabetes, you're at an increased risk of it regardless of how well you maintain your health.. . 2) Low Activity Levels and Obesity. . The reason overweight people are at such an increased risk for diabetes is that high blood pressure and cholesterol levels interfere with the body's ability to react properly to its use of insulin (which controls your blood sugar). Over time the blood sugar levels can get abnormally high, and this will result in a development of diabetes.. . There are also two types of the disease:. . With Type 1 diabetes, your body doesn't make insulin. With Type 2 (the more common type, which is explained by the above causes), the body makes insulin but is unable to use it.. . The causes of Type 1 diabetes are not currently known.. . I hope that helps.
