It depends on the culture you grew up in. In cultures influenced by Christianity, it is unlucky because that was the number of men at the Last Supper.. . However, in ancient Mesoamerica, the Aztecs and Mayans considered it sacred, and the number was very lucky in their calendars. For example, every trecana, or week, had 13 days, and the calendar cycle repeats itself every 52 years, or 4 x 13.
