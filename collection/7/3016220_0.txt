Gutlevel or Gut-level means at the deepest level.  Usually expressed in the phrase "Gut-Level Feelings.”   It seems to be more instinctive than pre-conscious intuition.. Gut-level is visceral.  Indeed, the word “gut” comes from the Middle English “guttes,” from Old English “guttas” meaning “entrails.”. Instincts include powerful motivations or impulses and talents or aptitudes that are unlearned abilities as well.. You are closest to your true self at your gut-level.
