Actually, insulin doesn't make it any harder to lose weight than pills. However, frequent exercise and a diet high in fiber, low in simple sugars, is the best way to control diabetes. Talk to a dietician who specializes in diabetes. There are diabetes care centers all over the U.S. Look in your phone book to see if there is one near you. If you dont' find one, call your local hospital and ask for a reference. it's worth it to pay for extra care in the long run!
