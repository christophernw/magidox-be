You don't know if someone is psychotic unless you are a license profession who is qualified to diagnosing an individual as psychotic.  You may find information in a variety of places to explain what psychotic is and what it means, however the book that is considered the Bible of mental disorders is the DSM-IV.  In order to find this very clinical manual, you should go to a bookstore, library or office of a clinical professional.  If you are concerned that someone is going to hurt someone or do something that is not in their best interest or the interest of others, then you should call the police and voice your concern or talk to a trusted adult or profession who can offer guidance on the situation, like an attorney or mental health professional.
