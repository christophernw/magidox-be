With your name and social one has access to a whole range of information and resources which can only be obtained by having this information.  Think of all of the times someone asked you at some business or institution for the last 4 digits of your SS# (bank, CC company, etc.)  With this info, and $20, a person can get a report containing every place you lived, what bills you pay, every alias you've had, bank, loan, credit card, and the names of your neighbors for the last 5 years.
