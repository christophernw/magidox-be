Empathy is putting yourself in their shoes and imagining how they might feel.  It can be about all emotion and thought--good and bad.. . Sympathy is feeling sorry for someone for how they feels but not always understanding their pain.  One could say it is understanding the emotion, but not really seeking answers about the cause.  It's usually related to negative feelings such as sadness and pain. . . Sympathy--I am sorry about your dog's death.. . Empathy--I can only imaganine how sad you must feel by losing Fluffy.  It must feel kind of lonely going on your morning walk.
