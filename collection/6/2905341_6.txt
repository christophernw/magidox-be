This recipe will make six slices of French Toast.. . 7 eggs. 4 tablespoons milk. 1 tsp vanilla extract. . whisk together and soak pieces of toast in mixture until well saturated on both sides.  Take straight from the egg mixture to a skillet.  Skillet should be heated to medium, with at least 2 tbs of melted butter.. . Sprinkle the exposed side of the toast with sugar and cinnamon and then flip.  Sprinkle the other side, let bread cook until it expands, flip one last time.  Cook for another 1 1/2 minutes.  . This will allow both sides to caramelize.  the exterior will be a little crunchy, and the inside will be soft and gooey.
