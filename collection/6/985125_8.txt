Verbal is okay and less than two weeks notice is acceptable.. A short polite resignation letter giving at least two weeks notice is standard. . e.g.. It has been a rewarding experience working here at ... I have chosen to explore other opportunites.  Please accept my resignation as of mm/dd/yyyy.. . Sincerely,. . . You may make it longer if you wish, but do not burn any bridges.  You want to be remembered as a valuable employee.. . Best of luck on your new career.
