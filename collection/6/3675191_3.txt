Its origin is Biblical from Genesis, where God cast Adam and Eve out of the garden and told them that they [and their offspring] would have to "work" the soil to provide for themselves.. . In essence, the saying means, we are all sinful and have to work for a living.  The saying probably had more meaning in past generations when more people went to church, and has now just become a thing that we say to indicate that we are busy.  :   )
