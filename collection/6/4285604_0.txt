A "light machine gum" (LMG) is one which can (if necessary) be carried and operated by one person.  "Assault rifle" is a generic term for a rifle which has the capability of laying down a base of fire in automatic bursts (usually of three to five rounds per discharge).  A "carbine" is a small rifle designed for use as a "secondary" arm for a combatant whose principal weapon is something other than the carbine OR as the principal defense weapon of a noncombatant.
