Good self esteem is feeling good about who you are. It means you believe you are just as good as any other person but not any better than anyone else. Arrogance is acting like you are better than others, but usually inside suffer from low self esteem. Braggers try to make themselves appear to be better to make themselves feel superior. It is usually a cover up for low self esteem. If you feel good about yourself you do not need to say and do things seeking attention.
