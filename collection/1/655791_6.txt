Nationalism is a sense of national consciousness based on awareness of being part of a community - a "nation" - that has common institutions, traditions, language, and customs and that becomes the focus of the individual immortality.. . Imperialism is the policy of extending on nation's power either by conquest of by establishing direct or indirect economic or cultural authority over another.  Generally driven by economic self-interest, it can also be motivated by a sincere (if often misguided) sense of moral obligation.
