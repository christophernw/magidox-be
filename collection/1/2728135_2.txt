javelin - a wooden or metal spear thrown for distance in track and field contests. it is at least 8 ft 6 3/8 inches long and at least 1.761 lbs in weight (used for sport). . spear - a weapon with a long shaft and a sharp, pointed head. spears are for throwing or thrusting with the hand in hunting, fishing, or warfare (used for killing). . think of it this way: all javelins are spears. not all spears are javelins.
