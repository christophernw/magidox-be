Jaipur as well as many of the other cities in rajasthan have nicknames by color. Jaipur is the Pink city bcoz all the buildings have a pinkish tint to them. Udaypur(another city in Rajasthan)is known as the white city bcoz the majority of the buildings are made of marble there. Jaisalpur is known as the Yellow city bcoz many of the buildings there are made of sandstone and therefore give off a yellowish tint.
