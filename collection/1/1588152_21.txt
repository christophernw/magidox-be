this recipe is for the white dhokla. now a days ready dhokla batter is available in stores get 1 kg batter n add . 1 table spoon oil . salt as per taste. add chilly paste. and add 1 tea spoon of baking soda . mix all this well n spread it in a dish sprinkle some pepper powder on it and keep in cooker (1/4 filled with hot water in it n put on gas)for baking keep it for 10 min . n your dhokla is ready
