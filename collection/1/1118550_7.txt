You could bake them in the oven: . . Get a baking tray, and place a sheet of foil on it - about twice the size of the tray. . Place the chicken breasts on the foil. . Make a parcel with the foil by bringing two of the opposite edges together and scrunching, then scrunch the two open ends (this will keep the steam in top stop the chicken from drying out). . bake in the oven at 200 C for about 25minutes, or until completely cooked
