Hydatidosis is a parasitic disease produced by a tapeworm (Cestoda) named Echinococcus granulosus.It has a global prevalence and relates to the closet of human and domestic animals. The parasite in mature form lives in the small intestine of dogs and many wild canine as the definitive hosts. Ova containing hexacanth embryo named onchosphere ,are excreted  by host feces. Then it is swallowed by intermediate hosts of parasite and hatched during the digestion process. Onchosphere passes through the intestine wall and peritoneal borders and gets home in many different organs such as liver ,lungs , peritoneum , kidneys , brain
