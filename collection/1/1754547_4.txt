By strong Will power and Determination you cultivate your mind and set in thinking positively.  Added to this pray to God regularly not only at fixed schedule timings, but always in your vacant moods.  Don't allow the mind to wander aimlessly and instead think about God and pray Him to give you the strength and wisdom. Read the Novels like Count of Monte Cristo (Alexander Dumas), Plays of Shekespere , Readers Digest, Wisdom, etc., and the like which not only improve your English knowledge but will make you wise and encourage you to be brave.  Forget about day dreaming.  God bless you.
