CCR5  is a genetic variant present in about 10% of Europeans that provides near-immunity from HIV progressing to AIDS when inherited from both sides.  A single copy of this gene can offer several extra years of health after infection.. This gene is present 10% in North American Whites, 2% in American Blacks, and 0% in African Blacks and Asians.. . This gene variant has been discovered to impair a persons ability to fight off West Nile virus, which may have led to it's absence in areas where that virus is common.
