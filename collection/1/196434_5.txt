Almost any dog, gets extremely attached to the person they are around the most. Chihuahua's are no different, in that respect, than any other dog. Many Chihuahua owners, tend to carry there dog around with them, like a handbag. This practice, makes Chihuahua's, look more like an accessory, than a pet. The Chihuahua is all dog, however, they love to play, have fun and have lots of attention payed to them.
