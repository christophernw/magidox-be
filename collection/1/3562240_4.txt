Why sift flour?  Because sifting aerates flour, giving it lift and even texture for ideal baking results.  Sifting helps to perfectly mix your flour, baking powder & soda.  It removes unappetizing lumps.  It is an essential tool when making cakes and other fine crumbed baked goods.. . Not sifting when it Calls for it in the recipe, results in you using More flour, etc than called for, and could result in Heavier texture.  Some flour is Presifted when you buy it.
