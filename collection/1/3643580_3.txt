Being defensive is more commonly associated with non-assertiveness. It is a response to a perceived threat. Non-assertion implies a lack of respect for oneself. Aggressive behavior implies a lack of respect for others. Assertive behavior is predicated on respect for oneself and others.. Keep practicing being assertive and you may see a decrease in defensiveness. Be open to confidence building experiences and avoid people who attempt to remediate their feelings of inadequacy by belittling others. . It can help to practice your assertiveness with someone who is also interested in learning how to be more assertive. You need the validation of accurate feedback. Validation reduces defensiveness.
