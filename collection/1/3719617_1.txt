There are many, many ways to relieve mental tension: meditation, massage, talk therapy, reading, writing, exercise, playing sports, sex, sharing your fears with a friend, prayer, playing games, painting, listening to music, playing music, vacation, swimming, scuba diving, making a life plan.. . The best thing you can do is find someone you trust and talk about your hell.  If you don't have someone in your life, then it is a great idea to talk to a mental health professional.
