Easy Sweet & Sour Chicken. . 1 Tbsp. oil. . 1 lb. boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces. . 2 cups frozen stir-fry vegetables. . 1 can (8 oz.) pineapple chunks in juice, undrained. . 1/2 cup KRAFT Sweet 'N Sour Sauce. . 1 cup water. . 2 cups MINUTE Brown Rice, uncooked. .  . HEAT oil in large skillet on medium-high heat. Add chicken; cook and stir 4 minutes or until cooked through.. . ADD vegetables, pineapple with juice and sweet and sour sauce; mix well. Stir in water. Bring to boil. . . STIR in rice; cover. Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer 5 minutes. Remove from heat. Let stand 5 minutes or until almost all of the liquid is absorbed and rice is tender.
