It sounds like you have a very routine life. Try spicing it up a little. If every day you do the same things over and over again, you're in a rut and the difference between a rut and the grave is about 6 feet.   .   Make a game out of your workday. Sincerely smile at everyone you met and then count how many smiles you get one back. Say "Good Morning" and really mean it and see what the reaction is. Watch to see how many times someone walks up to a door marked PULL and try to push on it. People can be very amusing, you just have to look for it..    Next Lunch break, don't sit at your desk, go outside and walk around. Get involved with a charity, theater group, or volunteer one night at a crisis center. When we give of ourselves, we become alive again.
