Hair Dryer, razor blade or scraper, and a cleaning product called "gunk".. . First use hair dryer or hot air gun (used to on heat shrink wrap) to heat sticker and soften adhesive.. . Then use razor or scraper to start at edge of sticker and peel off of surface.  Try to keep the sticker in one piece if possible.. . Finally, use "Gunk" to clean off the adhesive residue.
