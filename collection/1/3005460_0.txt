(1) Dunblane is one of main settlements of Stirling (unitary authority)north-central Scotland.. . (2) It is  bordered on the north by Perth and Kinross unitary authority, on the east by Perth and Kinross and Clackmannanshire unitary authority, on the south-east by Falkirk unitary authority, on the south by North Lanarkshire and East Dunbartonshire unitary authorities, on the south-west by Inverclyde unitary authority, and on the west by Loch Lomond and Argyll and Bute unitary authority. . (3) Stirling unitary authority came into existence on April 1, 1996, as a result of the Local Government etc. (Scotland) Bill 1994, which provided for the restructuring of local administration in Scotland
