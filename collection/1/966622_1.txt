Sleeptalking is also known as Somniloquy.  It can range from loud talking to low murmurs.  Listeners may or may not be able to understand what the person is saying.. . Sleep talking can occur by itself or as a feature of another sleep disorder such as:. .     * REM sleep behavior disorder (RBD) - loud, emotional or profane sleep talking.     * Sleepwalking.     * Night terrors - intense fear, screaming, shouting.     * Sleep related eating disorder (SRED). . Sleeptalking is very common and occurs in almost 50% of young children.. . It is usually not dangerous and should not be a cause for alarm.. . Hope this helps.
