The mother will usually take care of puppies, she should be secluded, in a quiet room, with a nursery box. There should be a heating pad, because puppies can't regulate their body temperature. But it can't be too hot, or it will hurt the puppies, or drive the mother away.  It's very important she be seen by a vet, she could have an undelivered puppy inside her, which could kill her. Keep puppies dry, warm,. and clean.  Do not let the male by her,even if he doesn't hurt the puppies, he will make the mother nervous
