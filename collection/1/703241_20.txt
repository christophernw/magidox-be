los·er (lū'zər) . n.. . One that fails to win: the losers of the game.. One who takes loss in a specified way: a graceful loser; a poor loser.. . One that fails consistently, especially a person with bad luck or poor skills: “losers at home seeking wealth and glory in undeveloped countries” (Arthur M. Schlesinger, Jr.).. One that is bad in quality: That book is a real loser.
