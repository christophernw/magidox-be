A culture's curse words are defined by what that culture holds most sacred and private. In our culture, for instance, our sacred and private things are what we do in. . 1) The bedroom. 2) the bathroom. 3) Church. . These three topics cover almost every curse word we've got. Since the incest taboo is pretty widespread, and sex happens in about every culture, it's not surprising that some incest related terms are curse words. As for why it's mother-son, hard to say. Could be that mothers tend to hold a more special place for a society's males, and so they are more more eligible to be cursed (why curse something no one cares about?).
