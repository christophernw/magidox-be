Jesus became the son of god in 325 CE in the council of Nicaea. The Roman Emperor Constantine in an effort to establish the burgeoning faith with its many interpretations gathered a consensus of religious men who decided on the divinity of Jesus and therefore made official the crede (the Triune God, ie concept of Trinity) that was espoused especially by the apostle Paul. . The divinitiy of Jesus, the Triune God is the essence of the Pauline branch of Christianity.
