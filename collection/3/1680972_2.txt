winnow. verb winnowed, winnowing. .       1. To separate chaff from (grain) by blowing a current of air through it or fanning it..             Thesaurus: sift, separate, divide, comb out, cull, select, fan, part.2. To sift (evidence, etc).. 1 to blow the chaff (= the outer coverings) from grain before it can be used as food. . 2 FORMAL to reduce a large number of people or things to a much smaller number by judging their quality:  A list of 12 candidates has been winnowed down to a shortlist of three.
