The characters used in the name for Japan are 日 ("ni" - also pronounced "hi", "nichi", "jitsu", "bi" or "ka") meaning Sun or Day, and 本 ("hon" - also pronounced "bon", "pon" or "moto") meaning Book, Long Thin Object or Origin.  The accepted meaning is the Origin of the Sun or more familiarly "The Land of the Rising Sun".  Accepted pronunciations are "Nippon" and "Nihon".
