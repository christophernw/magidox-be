At the last, the seventh trump, when the TRUE Christ returns to gather back together His own.  The end to this flesh AGE and the beginning of the millieum, the 1000 years when ALL will know the LORD; Then soon after, satan will be released and the ones Judged unrighteous will follow Him into the lake of fire and God will rid this earth of all evil elements.
