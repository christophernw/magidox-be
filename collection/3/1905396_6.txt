There are many, many political parties in the United States. See: http://www.politics1.com/parties.htm. . Traditionally, the two major parties, the Republican Party and those other guys are the general purpose parties with comprehensive platforms, and that attracts more voters.. . Most of the smaller parties tend to be single or a few issue parties that tend to attract only the most fanatical single-issue voters.. . Some of the "third" parties are beginning to attract more and more voters.
