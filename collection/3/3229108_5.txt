It's because we can develop very strong feelings for another human; we can love them so deeply and the next human, hate their guts. . It is not only how we feel about another being; but also linked to how one person feels; some people are angry or depressed all the time; they might be more prone to blame another human being than the tree in their garden or in the Amazonian forest. . . On the other hand, what has a tree ever done to man to be destroyed; Trees are our responsibility to protect whilst a human could defend himself. Trees have been here long before our race even existed, they provided us shelter and nutrition and nourished the earth that provided more; and maybe, subconsciously, some of us feel like trees are worth more respect than a human, who can make us feel a whole range of feelings from hate to passion to indifference and fear; and intolerance and injustice.
