take a pot drip a few drops of oil. cut half a handful of green onions put them in pot first turn on the heat. before onions start to burn, add two cans of chicken broth. add 1/2 can of water . chop 1 whole cucumber into thin round slices-add into pot. add mushrooms of your choice. add some corn starch----the more corn starch u add, the more stewy your soup. add salt. take 3-4 raw eggs and stir it with a fork till the yolk and white mixes completely. when the entire soup boils, you then slowly and carefully pour the raw mixed egg onto the surface of the boiling soup to make your eggs look like silk. . .
