Staphylococcus aureus is from the Greek: Staphylos = grapes and coccus meaning round shape, Aureus refers to golden as in Chemistry, Au is the symbol for Gold.. . Staphylococcus species are gram positive cocci and there are different  types: catalase positive, such as Staph Aureus, or catalase negative such as Staphylococcus epidermatis. . Each one causes different maladies and need to be cultured and identified in order for the clinician to prescribe the best course of treatment.
