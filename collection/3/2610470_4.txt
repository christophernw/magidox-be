It is the name of a street in south Manhatten.. In this street, the New York Stock Exchange is situated.. As a result, 'Wall Street' is often quoted as a synonym for the NYSE.. Also, the term 'Wall Street' is used in an even broader sense, including the brokers, analysts and all stock market operators. (Example: when you read 'Wall Street expects that...', this means that analysts are expecting something, even if they are not physically located in Wall Street)
