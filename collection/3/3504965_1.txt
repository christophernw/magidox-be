Silver Lake is a Los Angeles neighborhood east of Hollywood. Inhabited by a wide variety of ethnicities and socioeconomic groups, it is best known as an eclectic gathering of the creative community. Since the 1990s it has become the center of the alternative rock scene in Los Angeles. The Sunset Junction Street Fair, a major event in August, is held there. It also is -- or has been -- the home of musicians such as Beck, the Eels, Rilo Kiley, Pavement, The Red Hot Chili Peppers and the late Elliott Smith.. .  .
