The international Celsius scale sets the freezing point of water as 0°, and the boiling point of water as 100°.. The Fahrenheit scale (developed by and named after the German scientist Daniel Gabriel Fahrenheit) sets as 0° the lowest temperature ever recorded in Fahrenheit's birthplace Gdansk. He wanted to avoid negative temperatures in his scale. His own body temperature was set as 100°.. Thus the following relation between the Celsius and the Fahrenheit scales:. . Celsius in Fahrenheit = (( Celsius × 9 ) / 5 ) + 32. . Fahrenheit in Celsius = ( Fahrenheit - 32 ) × 5 / 9
