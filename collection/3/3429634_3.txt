Tangy Poppy Seed Fruit Salad . . A very light and delicious fruit dessert with a little twist.. Original recipe yield: 4 servings.. . INGREDIENTS:. 1 (20 ounce) can pineapple chunks - drained with juice reserved . 1 orange, peeled and segmented . 1 kiwi -- peeled, halved and sliced . 1 cup seedless grapes . 1 cup quartered strawberries . 1/4 teaspoon grated lime zest . 2 tablespoons lime juice . 1 tablespoon honey . 1 teaspoon poppy seeds . . DIRECTIONS:. In a large bowl, toss pineapple, orange, kiwi, grapes and strawberries. In a small cup or bowl, combine lime zest, lime juice, honey, poppy seeds and 1/4 cup reserved pineapple juice. Stir well and toss with fruit.
