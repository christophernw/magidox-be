Its not Spanish, is Nahuatl. . The name of Zihuatanejo comes from the Nahuatl word Cihuatlan that means "place of women", very probably because of this incursions of the king with all his wives who were a vast majority against the few men that safeguarded them.. Ixtapa is also Nahuatl and means "the white place" in reference to the white sands in the area.
