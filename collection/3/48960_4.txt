If you read Church history you'll see that there has almost always been some degree of controversy in the Church, going back to its earliest beginnings. The controversies have usually been over ecclesiastical authority and doctrinal issues. .  . The date of the East-West Schism is given in the encyclopedia as 1054, when the pope and the patriarch of Constantinople excommunicated each other.. . The date of the protestant Reformation is given as 1517, when Martin Luther nailed his 95 theses to the cathedral door.. . These dates are obviously an oversimplification; there was much history leading up to these events.
