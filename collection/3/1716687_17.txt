There are two passages of Scripture that come to mind. First is the one that says, "Search the Scriptures for in them ye think ye have eternal life. Second is the one that says, "I go before the throne of grace boldly." These references pertain to our ability to question with a humble heart in order to find the truth, so that we may proceed boldly to the Throne of God in order to petition God to help our prayers be answered, our witness to be proper, etc...
