A mirror is a reflective surface that is smooth enough to form an image. The best known example is the plane mirror. The most common use is in the home for shaving, make-up etc but mirrors are also used in scientific apparati such as telescopes and lasers, and in industrial machinery. . en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mirror. .
