Read a book in a subject that interests you, but about which you know little. . . As you read the book, keep a notebook with you. When you come to a word you don't know, write the word down in the notebook--in context from the book.. .  Look up the definition of the word and include it in the notebook. Include with context and definition the sample sentence from the dictionary. . . Continue reading other books and keeping the notebook. When your notebook is full. Reread it and practice using the words.. . NOTE: Pat Conroy is one author with an incredible vocabulary.
